About Me

I am passionate about the intersection of technology, social entrepreneurship, and psychology. From a young age, I’ve been a natural problem-solver—whether it was helping my dad fix my bike at nine or developing semester-based research capstone projects at my STEM October High School. While pursuing a STEM education, my passion for psychology continued to grow, and taking the AP Psychology exam reassured my desire to understand how people think and behave.
